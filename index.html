Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with a estimated 26 millions protestors

  2. Women's March in 2017 drew about 5.6 millions people nationwide

  3. March for Our Lives a student led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew about 2 millions people.

  4. Women's March in 2018 drew about 1.5 millions protestors

  5. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay, and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation drew about 1 million people in 1993
